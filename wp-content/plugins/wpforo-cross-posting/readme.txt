wpForo – Blog Cross Posting addon is designed to synchronize website/blog
content with forum content.When you create a new blog post, this addon creates
identical (title, content) topic in selected forum and keeps synchronizing these
entries.It connects the Blog Post with Forum Topic and Blog Comments with
according Forum Topic Replies. New comments under the Post are automatically
posted as replies under the connected (cross-posted) Topic and vice versa (new
replies of this Topic are automatically posted as comments under the connected
Post). These synchronization functions include Add/Edit and Delete actions.
 Addon also comes with rich set of options to manage all features.
